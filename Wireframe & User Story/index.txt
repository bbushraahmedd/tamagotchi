Tamagotchi: Keep your pet alive

From the users POV:
  1. Open to the home page
  2. Enter wtv name of your tamagotchi 
  3. Open to the start page where everything (all the tama’s needs) is at full capacity
      **Keep tama alive by feeding, playing, and letting it sleep
        a. Play
        b. Hunger
        c. Sleep
  4. When/If the levels on any of its needs lower, click on the button corresponding to the need to increase the level → keeping it alive
  5. If/When levels reach 0, tama has died and game is over
      **Have a button to where the player has the ability to start the game over
